# Shipping Policy

## Processing time
Orders are processed and dispatched within **1–2 business days**. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays begin processing the next business day. You will receive a confirmation email with tracking as soon as your order ships.

## Shipping methods and delivery times
We ship within the United States using the following options:

| Method | Delivery estimate | Cost |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Standard | 3–5 business days | Free on orders over $50, otherwise $5.99 |
| Expedited | 2 business days | $12.99 |
| Overnight | Next business day if ordered before 1 PM ET | $24.99 |

Delivery estimates are counted in business days after the order has shipped, not from the time the order is placed.

## Father's Day delivery
To guarantee arrival before Father's Day, place expedited or overnight orders by the cutoff dates posted on the homepage banner. Standard orders should be placed at least one week in advance.

## Order tracking
Once your order ships you will receive a tracking number by email. You can also ask the assistant "where is my order" with your order number to get the latest status.

## Shipping restrictions
We currently ship only to addresses within the continental United States, Alaska, and Hawaii. We do not ship to PO boxes for oversized items such as grills. International shipping is not available at this time.
