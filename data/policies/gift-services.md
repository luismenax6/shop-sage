# Gift Services

## Gift wrapping
Add premium gift wrapping to any item at checkout for **$4.99 per item**. Choose from several wrapping styles, including a Father's Day theme. Wrapped items ship in a protective outer box so the wrap arrives pristine.

## Gift messages
You can include a free personalized gift message of up to 250 characters. The message is printed on a card and tucked inside the package. Prices and receipts are never included in gifts.

## Gift receipts and returns
Recipients can return or exchange gifts without seeing the price by using the gift receipt. Gifts purchased during the Father's Day season (May 1 – June 30) enjoy an extended 60-day return window. The refund goes to the gift recipient as store credit unless they request a return to the original payment method.

## Engraving and personalization
Select products such as the insulated sports bottle and the leather hip flask can be laser-engraved with a name or short message. Personalized items take 1–2 extra business days to ship and are final sale unless they arrive damaged.

## Gift cards
Prefer to let dad choose? Digital gift cards are delivered by email, never expire, and can be applied to any order.
