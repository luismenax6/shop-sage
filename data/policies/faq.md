# Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I find a gift within my budget?
Tell the assistant your budget and who you are shopping for — for example, "a gift for my dad who camps, under $100" — and it will recommend products from our catalog that fit. You can filter by category, price, and what is in stock.

## Do you offer price matching?
We offer a one-time price adjustment if an item you purchased goes on sale within 7 days. We do not match competitor prices at this time.

## Is an item in stock?
Stock is shown on each product and the assistant can check it for you in real time. If something is out of stock, ask to be notified when it returns or request a similar in-stock alternative.

## Can I buy in bulk or for a corporate gift?
For orders of 10 or more of the same item, contact us through the assistant and we will create a ticket for our team to arrange bulk pricing.

## How do I track my order?
Ask the assistant "where is my order" with your order number, or use the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email.

## How do I contact a human?
If the assistant cannot resolve your issue, ask it to create a support ticket. A member of our team will reply by email within one business day. Support hours are Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM ET.

## What is your most popular Father's Day gift?
Grilling tools, multi-tools, and the whiskey stone set are perennial favorites, but the best gift depends on dad's hobbies. Just tell the assistant what he is into.
