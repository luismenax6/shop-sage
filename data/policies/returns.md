# Returns and Refunds Policy

## Return window
You may return most items within **30 days of delivery** for a full refund. Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and in resalable condition. We extend the window to **60 days** for gifts purchased during the Father's Day season (May 1 – June 30).

## How to start a return
1. Ask the assistant to open a return, or visit the Orders page.
2. Provide your order number and the item you want to return.
3. Print the prepaid return label we email you.
4. Drop the package at any carrier location within 14 days of receiving the label.

If your request needs a human, the assistant can create a support ticket and an agent will follow up by email within one business day.

## Refund timing
Refunds are issued to your original payment method within **5–7 business days** after we receive and inspect the returned item. Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

## Non-returnable items
For hygiene and safety reasons, the following cannot be returned once opened: consumable goods such as dry rubs, coffee, and beer-making ingredients. Personalized or engraved items are final sale unless they arrive damaged or defective.

## Damaged or defective items
If your item arrives damaged or defective, contact us within 7 days of delivery. We will send a replacement or issue a full refund including shipping, and you will not need to pay return shipping.
