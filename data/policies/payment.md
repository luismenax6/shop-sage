# Payment and Pricing Policy

## Accepted payment methods
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), as well as Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal. Gift cards can be applied at checkout and combined with one other payment method.

## When you are charged
Your card is authorized when you place the order and charged when the order ships. For pre-orders, you are charged only when the item is dispatched.

## Pricing and promotions
All prices are listed in US dollars and exclude applicable sales tax, which is calculated at checkout based on your shipping address. Promotional discounts cannot be combined unless stated, and price adjustments are available within 7 days if an item you bought goes on sale.

## Gift cards
Digital gift cards are delivered by email within minutes of purchase and never expire. They can be used on any product but cannot be redeemed for cash except where required by law.

## Security
Payments are processed through a PCI-compliant provider. We never store your full card number on our servers.
