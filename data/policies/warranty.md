# Warranty Policy

## Standard warranty
All products carry a **1-year limited warranty** against manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship, starting from the delivery date. This covers issues that occur under normal use and is in addition to your return rights.

## Extended and lifetime warranties
Certain brands offer longer coverage, which we honor:

- **Hand tools** (multi-tools, mechanic sets, BBQ tool sets) carry a **lifetime warranty** against breakage.
- **Electronics** (headphones, power banks, drones, turntables, GPS watches) carry a **2-year warranty** on the battery and electronic components.

## What is not covered
The warranty does not cover normal wear and tear, cosmetic damage, misuse, accidental damage, unauthorized repairs, or loss and theft. Consumable parts such as filters and batteries in non-electronic items are excluded.

## Making a warranty claim
To file a claim, ask the assistant to open a warranty ticket with your order number and a short description of the problem. Photos help us resolve claims faster. Approved claims are repaired or replaced at no cost; if a replacement is unavailable we issue store credit.
